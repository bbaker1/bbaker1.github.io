As a senior student-athlete at High Point University majoring in computer science while minoring in accounting, I am grateful for the rigorous academic and physical demands that continue to develop my strong work ethic.

Between class, homework, Division I lacrosse practice, weightlifting, team meetings, and community service, my day-to-day grind is full of opportunities for growth. However, during my time between one thing and the next, I get most energized by working on personal projects and spending time with my teammates.

While most of my coursework is in C++, I have also sought out projects to familiarize myself with Python and JavaScript, as well as both SQL and non-SQL databases. I have built applications using React/Redux, Python Flask, APIs, web servers, and backend databases. I have also worked with C# and Swift.

After graduating in May 2020, I feel my experiences and ability to thrive in a team-based environment will position me to be a capable Software Engineer in the tech industry. Once I have professional experience under my belt, I plan on pursuing graduate school to focus on the fast-paced field of Machine Learning.
